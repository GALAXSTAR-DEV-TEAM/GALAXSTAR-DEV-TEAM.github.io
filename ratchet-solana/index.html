The Game

Ratchet & Clank: Battle in Solana is an online fan game developed by the Galaxstar team.

Make use of a slew of returning weapons as well as all new never seen before guns blasting their way into Solana for the first time!

Battle across your favourite classic R&C planets like Daxx and Merdegraw in their online debut as well as completely original maps developed by our team, such as the Hydralis Seavault!

Re-discover classic maps featuring brand new textures, upscaled models and new map elements.

gamepad

Gameplay Experience

Battle in Solana’s gameplay is a blend of the classic UYA formula and FFA’s fast paced game modes, where you continuously attack your opponents base until all generators are destroyed.

Capture nodes using the all new customisable Omniwrench and choose your hero from a star-studded cast featuring the likes of Ratchet, Clank, Qwark, Talwyn, Rivet, Cronk, Zephyr and much much more!

All these will be available to purchase using bolts, Battle in Solena’s ingame currency. Everything in the game is obtainable completely for FREE and there are 0 microtransactions. There will be no pay to win and all players are equal!
